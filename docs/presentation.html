01 — Problem Statement

Reporting an issue is harder than it should be

  • Scattered contacts. Emergency and government numbers (112, 912, 2727, 3535, 9099…) live across dozens of separate websites and posters.
  • Wrong door, lost time. Citizens don't know which institution handles their problem, so reports bounce or never arrive.
  • No feedback loop. Once you call or report, there's little visibility into whether anything happens.
  • Projects stall. Delayed and unfinished works go unreported — until they make headlines.

01 — Problem · In The Headlines

This isn't hypothetical — it's front-page news

Accountability and unfinished projects are national conversations. IJWI turns that top-down pressure into a bottom-up tool — letting any citizen flag a stalled project or issue the moment they see it.

02 — Our Solution

IJWI: report, route, resolve — in one place

✎ Report an issue

Citizens submit a report with a description, photo, and location in seconds — no need to know which office is responsible.

☎ Unified numbers directory

Every verified emergency & government contact, with a "who do I call?" guide that maps a problem to the right hotline.

▤ Authority dashboard

Officials get a structured inbox of incoming reports instead of scattered calls and messages.

✦ AI summary digest

An AI-generated digest surfaces patterns and priorities so authorities can act on what matters first.
